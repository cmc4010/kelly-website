Process Section


July to September are the months of longan season. During the season we buy fresh longan from the local farms for production. The longan, by size, is placed in rooms to be dehydrated. The dehydration system uses the combination of water, furnace, and fans. After the dehydration process, the product is cooled and then goes through a washing process. The washing process is quick to prevent the softening of the longan shell. It is then placed in a silo in preparation for packaging. Our product comes in a box of 10kg. We use our own freezer as well as rented freezers to keep the products as fresh as possible. We use foreign boilers to efficiently dehydrate the product in a controlled manner. The company keeps up with the latest technology to produce the product of optimal quality. For the season of 2015, the company installed 6 more dehydration rooms for production and an improved boiler for better temperature control.
